President, Aligning Visions, S.A.
2005…

My passion for ecosystem conservation led me to create Aligning Visions to offer clients my skills in facilitation, program assessment, and organizational capacity building. Recent projects with great clients include:

  • Develop strategic plans, strengthen governance, and operationalize work plans using facilitated interviews and group retreats for clients such as:
    • Nature and Culture International – Strategic Plan 2020-2024
    • RedLAC – Strategic Plan 2020-2023
    • WWF Mexico –Strategic Plan 2018-2022
    • PACÍFICO – MigraMar Partnership MOU 2018
    • MigraMar nonprofit launch;
    • MAR Fund – Strategic Plan 2019-2023 and Reef Rescue Program design;
    • PACÍFICO Network Strategic Plan between environmental funds across four countries.
  • Lead independent project evaluations of conservation programs for donors and nonprofits. These highly participatory evaluations build in staff, board and partner reflections to develop findings, lessons learned, and future recommendations:
    • The Tropical Forest Conservation Act Program in Paraguay;
    • The Forever Costa Rica Trust;
    • The Brazilian Amazon Regional Protected Areas Program (FUNBIO and GEF);
    • The Mexican Natural Protected Area System (SINAP 2 with FMCN and GEF);
    • Tropical Forest Conservation Act Program in Guatemala (USAID); and
    • Climate Change Resiliency on the Mesoamerican Reef (WWF-UK).
  • Develop coaching and training programs that respond to client capacity-building requests:
    • Monitoring Program Impact — Nature and Culture International, Ecuador
    • Dynamic Project Design — Fundación Natura, Panama
    • Governance Best Practices — Caribbean-Pacific Environmental Fund Alliance
    • Chapter Capacity Building Program — Audubon California
    • Fundraising techniques for African Environmental Funds — RedLAC and CAFÉ
    • Evaluation Methodologies for GEF Accreditation — FUNBIO, Brazil
    • Governance and Launching a new NGO — Coral Triangle Center, Indonesia
    • Designing Innovative Conservation Projects — MAR Leadership Program, Mexico

In addition, clients often request case studies or the ability to document lessons learned in “how-to” style manuals or resource websites such as:

Director of Training, Latin America and the Caribbean, TNC
1992-1997

Built and managed a 10-person team to strengthen the institutional capacity of over 20 Latin American and Caribbean partner organizations implementing Parks in Peril – an international program dedicated to turning “paper parks” into well-managed conservation landscapes. We implemented organizational assessments, leadership training, capacity-building interventions, networking events such as the 400+ person Conservation Training Week bringing in practitioners from over 22 countries, and documented best practices.

Word of mouth  referrals  drive new clients to Aligning Visions. Clients are very satisfied with the high quality of my facilitation services, team-building processes, and well-written and feasible conservation planning and program assessment products.
